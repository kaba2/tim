Wang-Kulkarni-Verdu estimator
=============================

[[Parent]]: divergence.txt

The _Wang-Kulkarni-Verdu_ divergence estimator is a 
non-parametric estimator based on k:th nearest neighbors of sample 
sets.

References
----------

_A Nearest-Neighbor Approach to Estimating
Divergence between Continuous Random Vectors_, <br />
Qing Wang, Sanjeev R. Kulkarni, Sergio Verdu, <br />
IEEE International Symposium on Information Theory (ISIT), 2006.
